FAQ

Common questions

Are you open to speaking at virtual events?

Yes. I regularly speak at virtual summits and webinars. I prioritise events focused on Africa, Europe, innovation policy, and AI governance.

What's your availability for collaboration?

I'm in the early stages of my PhD, so I have selective availability. I prioritise collaborations that align with my research on entrepreneurial ecosystems, regional connectivity, and innovation policy.

Can you help review my startup pitch?

Generally no. My focus is on ecosystem-level dynamics rather than individual venture consulting. I'd recommend finding mentors with domain expertise in your specific industry.

Do you supervise or mentor research students?

Not formally at this stage. However, I'm happy to provide feedback on research ideas related to entrepreneurial ecosystems, innovation networks, or AI methodology through a coffee chat.
