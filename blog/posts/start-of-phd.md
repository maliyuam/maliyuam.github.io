# Beginning the PhD Journey at KU Leuven

*November 10, 2025*

I've officially moved to Belgium to join the **MSD (Managerial Strategy & Decision-Making)** research group at KU Leuven.

## Why This Group?
Prof. René Belderbos has built a world-class team focusing on the intersection of corporate strategy and innovation policy. It's the perfect place to explore my questions about ecosystem connectivity.

## The Road Ahead
Over the next 4 years, I plan to:
1. Build a comprehensive database of European founder mobility.
2. Develop new metrics for "ecosystem health" based on network topology.
3. Bridge the gap between academic network science and practical policy.

It's going to be a long journey, but I'm excited to share my learnings here along the way.
