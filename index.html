PhD Research Focus

Mapping the Hidden Networks of Innovation

Most innovation research treats ecosystems like isolated islands. My work reveals they're interconnected networks and those connections determine everything.

I am investigating how founders, capital, and knowledge flow between European tech hubs. This isn't abstract theory: these patterns will explain why some regions become innovation magnets while others hemorrhage talent.

My research combines systematic measurement of inter-ecosystem connectivity with analysis of entrepreneurial mobility patterns, revealing how startups arbitrage opportunities across regions and why traditional Silicon Valley models often fail when transplanted elsewhere.
