01 — About

Researcher. Builder. Bridge-maker.

I'm a PhD researcher at KU Leuven and Utrecht University, backed by a fully funded 4-year Research Council Doctoral Fellowship. My work maps the invisible bridges between entrepreneurial ecosystems — the founder migrations, co-invention networks, and capital flows that make some regions thrive while others stall.

Before the PhD, I earned an MSc at Carnegie Mellon, co-designed the "GenAI Inside" enterprise AI methodology published at IEEE AIxB, and founded two ecosystems of my own — Africa Innovators Society and Ignite Hub Africa — reaching 300+ students across two continents.
